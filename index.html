DRT — Debt Recovery Tribunal

The Debt Recovery Tribunal (DRT) is a specialized court where banks and financial institutions file cases to recover loans from defaulting borrowers. We represent both banks and borrowers before DRTs and the Debt Recovery Appellate Tribunal (DRAT) across Maharashtra & Goa.

  • Filing and handling of Original Applications (OA) by banks against loan defaulters
  • Representation of borrowers and guarantors to defend against unfair recovery proceedings
  • Filing of appeals before DRAT against DRT orders
  • Obtaining stay orders to stop auction or possession during litigation
  • Handling Securitisation Applications (SA) under SARFAESI Act Section 17 at DRT
  • Recovery of dues on behalf of banks through Recovery Certificates (RC) execution
  • Handling Interim Applications for attachment, injunction, and appointment of receivers
  • Advising banks on pre-litigation strategy and NPA classification matters

Title Search Report

Before buying property or approving a home loan, it is critical to verify the title of the property. A Title Search Report (TSR) is a legal document that confirms ownership, checks for encumbrances, disputes, and ensures the property is legally clear for sale or mortgage.

  • Ownership verification — tracing who owns the property and its complete history
  • Check for any existing loans, mortgages, or encumbrances on the property
  • Verification of court cases or litigation pending against the property
  • Review of government records — 7/12, Property Card, Index II, Revenue Records
  • Checking for unauthorized construction or violation of building permissions
  • Verification of chain of title going back 30 years as required by banks
  • Issuing a comprehensive TSR report for banks, buyers, and developers
  • Special TSR for SARFAESI auction properties and bank-held properties

SARFAESI Act Services

The SARFAESI Act (Securitisation and Reconstruction of Financial Assets and Enforcement of Security Interest Act, 2002) allows banks to recover NPAs without going to court. We are empanelled by nationalized banks as an authorized agency for Section 14 proceedings.

⭐ Bank Empanelled Agency

We are empanelled with nationalized banks to execute Section 14 SARFAESI orders — including 13.2 (Demand Notice execution) and 13.4 (Physical Possession Order) across all of Maharashtra & Goa.

  • Issuing Section 13(2) Demand Notices on behalf of banks to loan defaulters
  • Executing Section 13(4) Symbolic and Physical Possession of secured assets
  • Filing applications before District Magistrate / Chief Metropolitan Magistrate under Section 14
  • Managing and executing bank-possessed properties across Maharashtra & Goa
  • Representing banks in Section 17 objections filed by borrowers at DRT
  • Handling property auction process — publication, bidding, sale certificate
  • Advising on SARFAESI vs DRT strategy for optimal NPA recovery
  • Post-possession property management and custody services

Civil Law

Civil law covers disputes between individuals, companies, or organizations. Unlike criminal law, the goal is not punishment but resolution and compensation. Our team handles a wide range of civil matters across District Courts and High Courts in Maharashtra & Goa.

  • Filing and defending money recovery suits for unpaid dues, loans, and cheque dishonour cases
  • Handling contract disputes — breach of contract, non-performance, and damages claims
  • Filing Specific Performance suits to enforce sale agreements and contracts
  • Obtaining injunctions and restraining orders — temporary, ad interim, and permanent
  • Handling succession and inheritance matters — wills, probate, and Letters of Administration
  • Family law disputes including matrimonial cases, maintenance, and custody
  • Resolving commercial disputes between business partners or companies
  • Filing Section 138 NI Act cases for cheque bounce (dishonour) matters
